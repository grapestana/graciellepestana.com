Details about Proto-Personas:
What guided our next steps:

Each step was designed to ensure everyone’s voice was heard, especially the players, regarding what they liked and disliked about MH1, and we also included team members who had previously worked on MH1:

  • Since the project was under an NDA, we couldn’t directly interview players. Instead, we gathered data from Steam, Discord, and project surveys to collect all the information needed to build accurate player profiles. We also conducted an additional survey targeting MH1 fans who matched those profile categories.;
  • Typically, proto-personas are created using limited data and team assumptions. However, we aimed to make ours as close to real personas as possible. Since Mars Horizon 2 hadn’t been announced yet, the NDA prevented us from sharing information or interviewing players about the project.;
  • With the survey, we aimed to validate the accuracy and relevance of our personas and adjust any personality traits that didn’t align with our real players. This process helped us refine them until we could develop full, data-driven personas based on player interviews.;
  • With the full support and guidance of my UI/UX Director, I was able to design the entire process, share it with other UI team members for feedback, and deliver six profiles. Their collaboration was essential to ensuring everything ran smoothly and efficiently..
