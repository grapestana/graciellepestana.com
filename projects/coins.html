01
Briefing

We're working on a Bet system for Spades Jogatina, which is gambling on the basis, that players like to show off while playing, and it is a very traditional American game, played mostly by African Americans. One of the most famous competitions is Stance Spades which brings NBA players to the event while people play to get the trophies.

We need to create a coin system as part of this system that refers to all these details.

02
Process

The process steps were:


  • Benchmark of Adversaries on Store;
  • Benchmark of other Coin Systems in games;
  • We wanted the coins to glow and come with the same feeling as the NBA/NFL rings give to the champion players;
  • I've made a 3D version sketch of the coins;
  • Rewdrawn on Figma with the glows we wanted;
03
Results

The final version of the coin had:


  • A gold/orange colour to glow on the game screen;
  • A green diamond to bring the same essence of the NBA/NFL rings;
  • Various positions to help the coin transition on animations.
