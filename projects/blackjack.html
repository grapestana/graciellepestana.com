01
Briefing

The Blackjack project was a new game made by Gazeus Games as an addition to the Casino portfolio, and we needed a Logo and an Icon for the game launch. The idea was to bring a casino visual, coins and the icon should follow this guideline and be sized for Google Play and the App Store.

02
Process

I’ve brought the cards and coins elements to the Logo and Icon, following the requirements and based on the benchmarks, the process was:


  • Benchmark of Adversaries on Store;
  • Benchmark of Blackjack in real Casinos and on other Casino games as well;
  • Sketches with different positions and elements;
  • Colouring and final version of the Logo;
  • Study of Colours and Iconography to the Store Icons.
03
Post Production

After delivering the Logo and Icon we also made:


  • Screenshots with details for the Google Play Store;
  • Screenshots with details for the App Store;
  • Banners for Stores, and videos with VFX on After Effects as Ads on Facebook;
  • Ads and others campaigns to promote the game.
