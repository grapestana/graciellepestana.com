Benchmarks

Wireframes

We've done research with our marketing team about aesthetics that fit better with Spades, one of the concerns was bringing a game feel to the new aesthetic too. So, we've found that users that play Spades are more likely to have casino aesthetics.

After that, I tested two flows, A1 and B1 internally with five testers, and with the insights and difficulties, we made the last version with improvements A2 that worked successfully.







User Tests

We've done 5 internal tests with coworkers. The results showed that we need illustrations that represent each game length. So we chose to work with diamonds the smallest length got the diamond with fewer sides and the bigger length got more sides.
